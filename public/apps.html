Spinlist apps

Two free desktop apps that work alongside your Spinlist account. Sign in with your usual Spinlist email and password — your data stays on your account.

🎧

Music Manager

Scans your music library and syncs the track list to your account, so Prep can match song requests and export playlists that load straight into your DJ software.

  • Fast native folder scan — thousands of tracks in seconds
  • Reads your real file locations, so exported .m3u playlists work
  • Keeps your custom tag edits; your music files never leave your computer
⬇ Mac ⬇ Windows

📺

Gig Window

Opens your gig window — the night's key info, timeline and music — in a window that floats above your DJ software natively. No third-party pinning tools needed.

  • Sign in, pick tonight's gig, and it stays on top
  • Works for weddings and regular events
  • Switch gigs any time; refreshes itself through the night
⬇ Mac ⬇ Windows

🎚️

Crate Converter

Turns your .m3u / .m3u8 playlists — like the ones Prep exports — into Serato crates, ready to load. Just drag your playlists onto the window.

  • Drag & drop playlists → Serato crates
  • Writes straight to your Serato Subcrates folder
  • Flags any missing tracks so you can spot gaps
⬇ Mac

Quit Serato before converting, then reopen it to see your new crates.

🔒 Your privacy

These apps sign in with your Spinlist account and only read the data they need to do their job. The Music Manager sends your track list and file locations — never your actual music files.
