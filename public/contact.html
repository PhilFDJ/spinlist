Frequently asked questions

Do my guests need to download an app or sign up?

No. Guests just scan the QR code or open the link, then search and vote — no app, no account, nothing to install.

How do guests vote for songs?

They search live, pick their favourites, and their votes appear on a live leaderboard you can watch update in real time during the event.

Is there a free trial?

Yes — new accounts get 2 free events with all features included, no card required. After that you can subscribe to keep hosting.

What's the difference between BASIC and PRO?

BASIC is for regular hosts (a set number of events each month, up to 75 guests per event). PRO is for running events at scale, with unlimited events and guests plus playlist export. See the plans page for the full breakdown.

Can I export the voted songs to a playlist?

Yes (on PRO). You can export the song list and import it into Spotify, Apple Music or Tidal, so the crowd's picks become a ready-made playlist.

Can I add my own branding?

Yes — on paid plans you can add your logo, colour and tagline to the guest pages and exports, so it looks like your brand, not ours.
