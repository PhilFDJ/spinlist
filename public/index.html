Export playlist 🎵

Turn the voted songs into a playlist on Spotify, Apple Music or Tidal.

Step 1 — get the song list

The playlist file is a clean CSV (title & artist) that TuneMyMusic matches best. Or use Copy list to paste the songs directly.

Step 2 — import it

Open TuneMyMusic, choose Upload a file and pick the downloaded playlist CSV (or choose paste and paste the copied list), then pick Spotify, Apple Music or Tidal as the destination.
