Venue groups / parent companies
Set up the parent companies that own your venues (e.g. Away Resorts, Haven, Park Dean) and store their head-office email(s). You can then assign each venue to its group and send a group's whole lineup to head office from the Venue lineups section. Separate multiple emails with commas.
Demo controls
Reset the demo: permanently removes all venues, acts and their gigs so you can re-import a fresh demo set. Password-confirmed.
Photo & video uploads (cloud storage)
Connect your cloud storage so your acts can upload photos and videos from their check-off page. They land in your storage under “GigConfirm Uploads”, in a folder per act — ready for your social media. Only the agency head can connect or disconnect.
Import gigs from Google Calendar
Connect your Google Calendar to pull gigs straight from it instead of uploading a CSV. Events read as “Act @ Venue” (or the calendar name is the act). Only the agency head can connect.

Venue contacts?

Add or update venue contact details. Accepts CSV, Excel, Word or PDF. We'll read what we can and show it for you to check before saving — nothing is saved until you click Save. To bulk-edit, export to CSV, edit in Excel, and re-upload.

Send this week's confirmations?

Load this week's gigs by uploading a bookings CSV or importing from your Google Calendar. Each act gets one email with a link to confirm all their gigs, after a review step. Venue contacts are matched by venue name — manage them in the section above.

Review before sending —

Each act is listed with their gigs. Leave a gig ticked to include it in that act's confirmation email; untick to hold it back (it's marked flagged, not sent). The share contact tick on each gig controls whether the act sees that venue's phone/email — this saves as the venue's default for next time.

▸ Saved venue contacts?

These are stored on the server and reused every week. Edit a row and click Save, or remove a venue you no longer need.
